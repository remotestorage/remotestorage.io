Add remoteStorage support to your platform

To add remoteStorage support to your online storage product, please follow our latest IETF Internet Draft: draft-dejong-remotestorage-01.

NB: keep in mind is this warning about 412 response codes.

All existing providers are still using one of the previous two versions, draft-dejong-remotestorage-00 and 2012.04, but 2011.10 is now deprecated.
